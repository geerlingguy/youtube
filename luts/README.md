# Jeff Geerling - LUTs

This folder contains LUTs I use (`.cube` files) to grade 'RAW' footage from various cameras.

The LUTs are organized by camera, since they are specific to that camera. Most LUTs are sourced from camera makers directly, and follow whatever licensing that camera manufacturer chooses.
